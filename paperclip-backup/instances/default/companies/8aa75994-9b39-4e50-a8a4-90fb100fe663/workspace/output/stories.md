## 5. Juries Find Meta and Google Liable in Child Harm Social Media Trials

Two separate U.S. juries found Meta and Alphabet's Google liable in cases tied to harm to children on their platforms—one in California, one in New Mexico—with a Los Angeles jury ordering a combined $6 million in damages. The verdicts set the stage for a landmark legal battle over the scope of platform liability protections and could reshape how Big Tech moderates content for minors.
